This analysis is for BEAST 2[1].
There is one alignment with 17 taxa, and 3221 characters.
The partition has a gamma site model and HKY substitution model [2] with kappa log-normally distributed (mean-log= 1.0 and stdev-log= 1.25) and estimated frequencies.

The partition has an uncorrelated log-normal relaxed clock [3] with clock rate 2.0E-5 and standard deviation gamma distributed (alpha= 0.5396 and beta= 0.3819).

There is a single tree with dated tips (in dates not ages) using constant coalescent tree prior with population size gamma distributed (alpha=0.01 and beta=100.0).
